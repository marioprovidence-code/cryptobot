🎓 UNDERSTANDING THE INDICATORS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SMA (Simple Moving Average):
├─ Yellow Line: 20-period (recent trend)
├─ Red Line: 50-period (overall trend)
├─ Cross-over: Golden cross = bullish
├─ Cross-under: Death cross = bearish
└─ Usage: Identify trend direction & support/resistance

RSI (Relative Strength Index):
├─ Range: 0-100
├─ > 70: Overbought (potential bearish reversal)
├─ < 30: Oversold (potential bullish reversal)
├─ Middle (50): Neutral zone
└─ Usage: Identify extreme conditions & reversals

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence):
├─ Shows momentum & trend changes
├─ Histogram: Positive = bullish, Negative = bearish
├─ Signal Line: MACD crossover = potential entry/exit
└─ Usage: Confirm trend changes

VOLUME:
├─ Green bar: Buying pressure (close up)
├─ Red bar: Selling pressure (close down)
├─ High volume: Strong move (more reliable)
├─ Low volume: Weak move (less reliable)
└─ Usage: Confirm price moves with volume

📈 TRADING STRATEGIES USING INDICATORS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SCALP TRADING (1-5m timeframe):
├─ Watch: RSI for 30/70 extremes
├─ Confirm: Volume surge
├─ Entry: RSI bounce from 30/70
├─ Exit: Quick profit (0.5-1%)
└─ Ideal: 1m, 5m timeframes

SWING TRADING (1h-4h timeframe):
├─ Watch: SMA crossovers (golden/death cross)
├─ Confirm: MACD histogram
├─ Entry: Price above both SMAs with volume
├─ Exit: When SMA crosses bearish
└─ Ideal: 1h, 4h timeframes

DAY TRADING (5m-1h timeframe):
├─ Watch: RSI levels + SMA breaks
├─ Confirm: Volume confirms move
├─ Entry: Break above/below SMA with RSI confirmation
├─ Exit: Target 2-3% profit or opposite signal
└─ Ideal: 5m, 15m, 1h timeframes

POSITION TRADING (1d-1w timeframe):
├─ Watch: Long-term SMA (50/200)
├─ Confirm: Weekly MACD
├─ Entry: Pull back to SMA after uptrend
├─ Exit: When breaks long-term support
└─ Ideal: 1d, 1w timeframes
